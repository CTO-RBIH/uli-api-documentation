The Legal Verification API provides comprehensive legal background checks for both individuals and entities. It checks and retrieves legal history, case details, and compliance status from multiple sources including Supreme Court, High Court, e-Court, Consumer Court, FIRs, and defaulter lists. Also provides arrested, most wanted, and absconder data.
